Quckie: open Skype links in right browser

Mi 12 Juni 2013
By sulami

In Web.

tags: quickiechromefirefoxskypexdg

I currently use Google Chrome as my main browser on Linux because I need the 11.7 Flash plugin. The downside was that I could not open links in Skype conversations by clicking them, as they would just open in Firefox, even though Chrome is my default browser. The solution on my openSUSE box was quite simple:

~ > % xdg-settings get default-web-browser
firefox
~ > % xdg-settings set default-web-browser google-chrome.desktop
