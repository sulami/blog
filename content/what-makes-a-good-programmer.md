Date: 2015-04
Category: Coding

Status: draft

This is a very interesting question that I have been asking myself lately. I am
a mostly self-taught programmer, but recently I have gotten the opportunity to
go to university and study CS. This is something that puts me in a dilemma,
because going to university would mean, I would miss out on money I am
currently making, because I could not work full-time and study at the same
time. But people usually say, you will not regret getting a traditional degree.
Also, while a lot of traditional material is not really what I would call close
to reality, but fact is, a lot of more sophisticated topics are only discussed
in those circles.
