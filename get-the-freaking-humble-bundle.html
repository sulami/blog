Get the freaking Humble Bundle!

Mi 19 Februar 2014
By sulami

In Games.

tags: gaming

If you are a Linux gamer like me (although I barely play games anymore as I don't have the free-time I used to have anymore), get the Humble Bundle. For some dollars you get 6 great games, 5 with native Linux-support, one that will get a native Linux version later this year for free, all the soundtracks as mp3s and flacs, and there is hidden stuff which will be unlocked later on. What are you waiting for?
