>> Synchronizing Bookmarks and more across different browsers

Fr 31 Mai 2013 | in Web | tags: chrome firefox xmarks

I prefer different browsers depending on which platform I am. On Windows, nothing really beats Firefox, but on Linux, I prefer Chrome because it is significantly faster and uses a newer Flash version. But also important for me are the dozens of bookmarks, which have get synchronized automatically, which I realize with Xmarks. Xmarks provides browser addons for the popular browsers and can sync your bookmarks, history, passwords and even your currently opened pages. I have used it for some years now and am completely satisfied, so if you could need it, give it a try.
