>> Kudos to OCZ

Di 21 Januar 2014 | in Hardware | tags: ssd

Recently my SSD, an OCZ Vertex 3 MaxIOPS just stopped working, not that big of a deal, I've got backups of most of my stuff and hardware failure occures sometimes, nothing we can really do something about. But when I sent it to the retailer I got it from, Mindfactory, they told me I would have to proof, the damage wasn't caused by me. I have no clue how they think this is supposed to look like.

So I got it back and contacted OCZ directly, who answered my support emails within some minutes and then sent me an address to send my SSD to. Yesterday I received a refurbished one, same model, including all the supplies I didn't send them, and even a new sticker. For free. This is customer support.
